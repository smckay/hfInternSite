FAQs
I know nothing about health care. Will I still be a good fit at Healthfirst?
Absolutely not. We expect interns to know all the ins and outs of all healthcare legislation passed in the last century. Anything less is unacceptable and you should not bother applying
What percentage of interns are given return offers for future internships or full time positions?
200% of interns get return offers of some kind. That's right, every itern not only gets to return to Healthfirst, but they also get to choose any other person they know to come work at Healthfirst.
What is the dress code for interns?
All interns are expected to wear full suits every day. Anything less will result in immedate termination and blacklisting from every job you ever apply to.
What types of events are held for interns?
No events are held for interns. We do not expect or allow them to get along together in any capacity.
