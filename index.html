About the Intern Program

Each Summer, we welcome students from many prominent colleges and universities for the Healthfirst Summer Trailblazer Program.

Interns come to Healthfirst to learn about our unique business model, as well as to have an impact on our organization and our members. Our program is known for providing valuable career experience and leadership development.

To be eligible for the 10-week paid program, students must be enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate program at an accredited institution, or have graduated within 12 months of the program start date.

About Healthfirst

Healthfirst is a provider-sponsored health insurance company that serves more than 1.2 million members in downstate New York. Healthfirst offers top-quality Medicaid, Medicare Advantage, Child Health Plus, and Managed Long Term Care plans. Healthfirst Leaf Qualified Health Plans and the Healthfirst Essential Plan are offered on NY State of Health, The Official Health Plan Marketplace. Beginning in 2017, Healthfirst is offering Healthfirst Pro and Pro Plus, Exclusive Provider Organization (EPO) plans for small-business owners and their employees, and Healthfirst Total, an EPO for individuals.

Our Mission

Our members are our North Star and our mission is guided by their needs and preferences in ensuring a superior experience and access to the highest quality healthcare when and where they need it.

Our Values

Members First

We exist to serve and respect our members. Their satisfaction and well-being is the best barometer of our success. Further, we must align with the communities we serve and be a responsible community member.

Quality Execution and Continuous Improvement

We chase perfection in order to catch excellence and we know we cannot rest on past success. Excellence, willingness to change, and continuous improvement are our only paths to the future.

Employee Success

Our employees are our greatest asset. We strive to create an environment where every employee has the tools, feedback, and opportunity to excel and grow professionally. The success of our employees enables the success of Healthfirst.

Cooperation

Our success depends on our ability to overcome challenges better and faster than our competitors. To do this, our employees at all levels must work together as a team regardless of their functional departments.

Integrity and Transparency

We work at the highest level of openness, integrity, and compliance. We honor our commitments and keep our promises. Transparency fosters trust.

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need a healthcare background to be considered?
No, a healthcare background is not required, but may be preferred in some roles.
How many students apply for the Summer Trailblazer Program?
In 2017, we had more than 7000 applicants for our Summer Trailblazer Program.
What is the interview process?
The interview is a two-step process starting with the completion of an online HireVue. If the applicant is selected to move forward after their HireVue, they'll be invited to participate in an in person interview with the Summer Trailblazer Program Manager, as well as leaders from the applicable department.
How much are interns paid?
Intern pay depends on education level - undergraduate students are paid $20 an hour, while graduate students are paid $25 an hour.
Does Healthfirst offer housing for the program?
While Healthfirst does not offer housing, there will be resources available to interns to help them find housing for their summer in New York City.
What will I be doing during my internship?
As a Summer Trailblazer, you'll be exposed to a number of different opportunities. For starters, you'll participate in the day-to-day functions for your business area. You'll also have the opportunity to hear from our business leaders about their career experiences in weekly roundtable sessions. Finally, you'll be assigned an individual project focused on solving a real business problem.

How are mentors matched with interns?
Mentors and interns are matched based on career focus as well as personality and interests.
Does each intern have his or her own individual mentor?
Yes, a major focus of the Summer Trailblazer Program is to create a personalized connection between the mentors and mentees to provide a more engaging experience for our interns.
What kind of support can I expect from my mentor?
Your mentor will be your guide throughout the program; he or she will share their professional experience and will provide resources to help your success as a Summer Trailblazer. Mentees can expect to have regular interactions with their mentors including, but not limited to, daily check-ins and weekly one-on-ones to discuss career development.
What will my mentor expect from me?
Mentors have found that the best mentor/mentee relationships start with the following traits:
  • Positive attitude
  • Openness to try and learn new things
  • Ability to be a team player and collaborate with others
  • Willingness to take chances
What kind of opportunities are there to network?
As part of the Summer Trailblazer Program, we believe it's not only important for our interns to network with each other, but also with their mentors and Healthfirst leaders from different levels, so we host three social events throughout the summer to allow for networking outside of the workplace. In addition to the social events, interns also attend weekly roundtables, where they have the chance to ask questions and network with members of the leadership team
How often do interns receive offers for full-time positions at Healthfirst?
In 2017, of the 20 interns eligible for full-time employment, 11 received and accepted full time offers.
